What can I do with SynthLab?

01 Creative Learning of Music Theory

The application provides music theory lessons covering fundamental concepts such as scales, chords, sheet music reading, as well as the structure and components of various musical genres.

02 AI-Powered Melody Generator

To support music producers in their creative process, the application incorporates an AI-based melody generator.

03 Virtual Synthesizer Simulator

The application offers a comprehensive virtual synthesizer simulator that includes all essential parameters for learning sound synthesis.

MIDI Keyboard Integration

Users can connect a MIDI keyboard to the application for practical music theory learning. The application will recognize notes played in real-time, enabling interactive learning and effective ear training.
